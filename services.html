Support Services

Helpful services for students, families, and visitors

Available Services

Flower Art Academy offers support services to make enrollment, attendance, performances, and communication easier for our students and families.

  1. Enrollment and Scheduling

    • New student registration assistance
    • Private lesson scheduling support
    • Class placement guidance by age and level
    • Hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

  2. Student and Family Support

    • General questions about programs and pricing
    • Attendance updates and class change requests
    • Allergy-related accommodation questions
    • Hours: Monday to Thursday, 10:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.

  3. Performance and Event Support

    • Information about recitals, showcases, and theater events
    • Help with event arrival times and student preparation
    • Graduation celebration details, including the sunflower tradition
    • Hours: Friday, 12:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. and Saturday, 9:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.

  4. Facility and Visitor Assistance

    • Front desk help for first-time visitors
    • Directions to classrooms and studios
    • Building access information during workshops and events
    • Hours: Saturday, 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.

Additional Community Support

Families visiting the academy may also benefit from nearby dining, parking, and local community resources during performances, workshops, and extended lessons.
