Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers for students, parents, and visitors

FAQs

What ages do you serve?

We welcome children starting at age 5, teens, and adults depending on the class type and level.

Do you offer trial classes?

Yes. First-time students may request a one-day trial class to experience the program before enrolling.

Are private lessons available?

Yes. We offer private lessons for selected music and visual arts programs based on instructor availability.

What should students bring?

Some classes provide materials, while others may require students to bring a notebook, instrument, or art supplies.

How do I enroll?

Families and students can use the contact page to ask questions or request enrollment information.

Do you host events or performances?

Yes. Theater performances, recitals, and special workshops are offered during the year depending on the program.

Policies

Flower Art Academy encourages respectful participation, on-time arrival, and advance notice for schedule changes whenever possible. Program materials and class placement may vary by age and skill level.

Graduation Tradition

At Flower Art Academy, we celebrate our students' growth and creativity with a special graduation tradition. Each graduating student receives a sunflower to represent their artistic journey and personal growth.

A real sunflower is provided whenever possible. For students with allergies, a realistic artificial sunflower is offered so every student can participate in this meaningful moment.

Still Have Questions?

We are happy to help with enrollment, scheduling, and program details.
