About Our Academy

Flower Art Academy is a family-owned creative arts school offering inspiring classes in music, theater, and visual arts for children, teens, and adults. We believe creativity grows when students feel supported, encouraged, and challenged.

Our Programs

Music

Private and group lessons in guitar, piano, and more.

Theater

Stage performance, acting, and creative expression.

Visual Arts

Painting, drawing, and mixed media classes.

Why Choose Us

We provide small class sizes, experienced instructors, and a welcoming environment where students can grow creatively and confidently. Graduating students receive a sunflower to celebrate their artistic journey.
